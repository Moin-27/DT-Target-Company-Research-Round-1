# DT Target Company Research — Round 1

This is my submission for the Business Analytics Internship assignment. The task was to identify 25 companies that fit DeepThought's "Federer Company" profile, evaluate them using a structured scoring framework, and put together a plan for how I'd scale this to 1,000 companies.

## What's in This Repo

- **target_companies.csv** — The main research output. 25 companies with full scoring, decision maker details, verdicts, and personalization hooks.
- **methodology.md** — How I approached the research: where I looked, how I screened companies, and what I found along the way.
- **1000_company_proposal.md** — My plan for scaling this process to build a 1,000-company ICP list.
